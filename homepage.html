Welcome to Wisconsin Lens

About

Wisconsin Lens is a website dedicated to showcasing the beauty and diversity of Wisconsin. Our mission is to provide a platform for photographers and artists to share their work and contribute to the Wisconsin Lens community.

Galleries

Our galleries serve as a showcase for the Wisconsin Lens community's work. Explore and enjoy the beauty and diversity of Wisconsin through the eyes of our talented contributors.

Submit Photo

We welcome submissions from photographers and artists who want to share their work with the Wisconsin Lens community. To submit a photo, please follow these steps:

  • Ensure your photo meets our submission guidelines.
  • Upload your photo to our submission form.
  • Provide a brief description of your photo.
  • Submit your photo for review.
